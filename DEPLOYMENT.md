### Admin Instructions:
1. Go to `/admin/blog`
2. Click "New Post"
3. Upload featured image (drag & drop supported)
4. Write content using simple markdown:
   - `## Heading` for sections
   - `**bold**` for emphasis
   - `- item` for lists
5. Click "Show Preview" to see how it looks
6. Click "Save Post" to publish
